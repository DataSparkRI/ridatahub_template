FAQs

How do I change the indicators on a WEAVE tool?

Click on the name of the indicator you wish to change. From this new window, remove the old indicators, browse through the many data sets available, and choose the new indicator you are interested in viewing.

How can I create my own Data Story?

If you are interested in creating your own Data Story we would love to hear from you. Please contact questions@ridatahub.org.

Who do I contact if I think I've found a bug in the software?

Please contact questions@ridatahub.org if you think that the software is not performing as it should.

Who creates Data Stories?

Data Stories are created through a collaborative process with community leaders, topical experts, and the Providence Plan. Community partners develop the topics and guiding questions for the data investigation. The Providence Plan provides technical assistance and sometimes acts as facilitator in the story making process.

can I request a topic for a new Data Story?

Please send your ideas for new Data Stories to questions@ridatahub.org. While we are always interested in new ideas for data stories, due to staffing limitations we cannot always accommodate requests.
